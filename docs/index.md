# FNA Adjuster: Align with Math, Refine with Feel

> **Stop struggling with coordinate systems. Start placing objects with professional ease.**

**FNA Adjuster** is a high-precision transform utility designed to eliminate the friction of object placement in Blender. Whether you are a hard-surface modeler needing perfect alignment or a set-dresser looking for a more tactile way to layout scenes, this tool bridges the gap between mathematical accuracy and artistic intuition.

## What does "FNA" stand for?

**FNA** stands for **Face Normal Align**.
It represents the core DNA of this tool: extracting perfect rotation data directly from your geometry's faces to ensure your objects are always oriented exactly as they should be.

## Why FNA Adjuster?

In standard Blender, aligning an object to a specific face and fine-tuning its position often requires a complex sequence of keyboard shortcuts and menu toggling. FNA Adjuster simplifies this into a single, fluid workflow. Extract the rotation from any face, then use our unique Jog-Dial system to "tune" your object into place.
